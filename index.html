Design Features

Seven features. Two printable parts.

An FDM-printable top-case shell that replaces the original Pebble M350s flat top, plus a clip-on wheel cap. Magnetically attaches — no tools needed.

01
⌒

Ergonomic Palm Dome

A gentle raised curve across the palm area reduces wrist extension and fatigue during long sessions, without making the case bulky.

Comfort · Posture
02
◎

Wheel Extender Cap

A separate clip-on cap presses onto the mouse's real scroll wheel and enlarges it (~11mm → 16mm). It spins with the wheel, so it can be turned by thumb, knuckle, or palm — not just a fingertip. Reversible, no glue.

Accessibility · Separate part
03
⊔

Wrist Support Ledge

13mm rear ledge catches the wrist and prevents the mouse from sliding. Reduces the need for repeated repositioning.

Stability · Tremor control
04
≡

Side Grip Ribs

5 ribs per side press-grip the thumb and ring finger. Increases lateral force without needing a tight squeeze — helps with low grip strength.

Grip · Low-dexterity
05
⠿

Tactile Button Dots

Four raised dots on the left-button zone allow users to locate the click surface by touch — no need to look down at the mouse.

Tactile · Visual impairment
06
▭

+5mm Button Extension

A forward lip extends the button surface 5mm toward the user — shorter finger reach for clicking, useful for users with reduced finger extension.

Reach · Dexterity
07
⬡

3-Magnet Pockets

Triangle layout — front-left, front-right, rear-centre — matches the Pebble M350s base. N52 disc magnets press-fit in. No glue, no screws, no tools.

Magnetic attachment · MAS-1 standard

Quick print guide

The STL files download ready to print — no OpenSCAD or CAD steps needed. Just slice and go.

  1. Top case — open pebble-top-case.stl in Bambu Studio or PrusaSlicer
  2. Set PETG · 235°C nozzle · 85°C bed · 0.2mm layers · 3 walls · 20% gyroid
  3. Supports under the wrist-ledge overhang only — print top face down
  4. Press 3× N52 magnets (6mm × 3mm) into the three underside pockets — no glue
  5. Wheel cap — print scroll-wheel-cap.stl (PLA/PETG, 0.12mm, no supports — a few minutes)
  6. Pop the mouse's scroll wheel off its clip, slide the cap on, click the wheel back
  7. Snap the top case onto the Pebble M350s base — magnets self-align. Done.
