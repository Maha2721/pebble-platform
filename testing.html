Accessible Scroll — Proof of Concept Assessment

Scroll Accessibility Challenge

This activity measures scroll accuracy, precise control, and performance during repeated scrolling — to help classify what kind of scroll support you may need.

Use only the mouse scroll wheel. The process includes calibration, a short practice, an adaptive game, and a self-report.

How it works

Scroll up to move the Logitech mouse icon upward.

Scroll down to move it downward.

Guide the mouse through the gap in each wall.

The activity takes about one minute. The practice round is not scored.

Calibration

Use the scroll wheel to move the mouse icon to the highlighted target on screen.

No data is recorded during calibration.

Practice round

Complete three walls with large openings. No score is recorded here.

Adaptive game — ready

The next 20 obstacles will be measured.

After two consecutive successes the opening narrows. After one miss it widens. This adapts to your natural scroll range.
